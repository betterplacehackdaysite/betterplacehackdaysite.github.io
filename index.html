Online Fundraising Hackathon

Berlin: 19th-21st July 2013

Why and What is it?

Online dontation and Fundraisning are becoming increasingly important in for the NGOs. There hasn't past much time, since the first online donation App or platform was build and their has happen a lot. But how will the future look like? At the Digital Donation - Hack Days, we want to bring social entrepreneurs, creative people, tech people, business people and experts together to develop and discover the future of digital dontations with you. Together we want to create innovativ new tool, features or concepts.

Dicuss the problems, pitch your ideas, form a team, work, get feedback from experts, mentors or other teams and finally present what you have achieved in 3 days. Create Apps, Websites or new concepts! Get to know like minded people, do good, exchange knowledge, learn, build and network in a collaborative atmosphere.
